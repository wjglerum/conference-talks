Willem Jan Glerum

Principal Software Engineer at Lunatech, based in Delft.

Started on the JVM with Scala, Play and Akka, building reactive systems. These days I work on cloud-native applications with Quarkus and modern Java. My talks tend to circle around two themes: concurrency, where the trade-offs between reactive programming and virtual threads are still being worked out, and AI agents, where Quarkus LangChain4j and friends meet the realities of OIDC, guardrails and tool calling.

Off-keyboard you'll find me volunteering at Scouting events or somewhere outdoors.
